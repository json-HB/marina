Who is KreditOne?

 KreditOne Sri Lanka is part of KreditOne group, a digital consumer finance marketplace who connect lenders and borrowers and company providing short term credit to consumers. We are a multinational company and we  launched our business in 2019. Our company is one of the most trustworthy and it has the protected ways to take short term loans for  unforeseen needs. We have come up with this bold new means of providing loans, which is not only new but the simplest, fastest and most secure way of borrowing money for consumers in Sri Lanka.

In this new fintech consumer finance landscape, we have gone beyond excessive paperwork, meetings and phone calls, followed by long and uncertain waiting period for your loan approval to come through. These loans are designed to meet all your short term financial needs such as medical emergency or any other personal needs.

KreditOne aims to provide cutting-edge practices in the digital financial services space in Sri Lanka with new innovative practices to help people to manage life's everyday expenses.
